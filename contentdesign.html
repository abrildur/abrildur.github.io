Content Design Portfolio

La Nuit & Co

My freelance experience has included the management of publications for a startup called "La Nuit & Co", which is a startup whose services and products are related to the esoteric and spiritual. In addition to content creation on social networks (TikTok, Instagram and Facebook), I also managed their social networks (TikTok) and the landing page prototype for this company can also be seen in other sections of my portfolio.

This section showcases some of the work I did for La Nuit & Co, which had a remarkably wide reach.

Brief summary

The strategy for increasing the reach of the brand on social networks was to create content based on the preferences of current and future customers of La Nuit & Co. As a consequence of the results obtained from certain methods of UX research, one of the characteristics that we considered is that the customers of La Nuit & Co hold beliefs in the spiritual, mystical, and esoteric.

About the post #1: Luna Azul. (Blue moon)

Using this information, we began to create content. In honor of the blue moon, one of the nearest mystical events, we created content which describes the moon's history, what makes it so mysterious, and a ritual related to that moon.

With this strategy, we gained more than 1000 followers in one night and the video has more than 11k views on tiktok.

About the post #2: Sintomas de la brujeria: "cierre de caminos" ( Symptoms of witchcraft: "road closure").

We decided to create the following content related to witchcraft and its possible symptoms based on the tastes of the potential customers of La Nuit & Co.

We gained 1200 followers in 1 day and a half as a result of this TikTok post, and the post has garnered over 24k views.
