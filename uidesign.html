La Nuit & Co (Web Desktop page)

La Nuit & Co

The prototype developed for a local Monterrey company called "La Nuit & Co.".

The company is located in Monterrey, Nuevo Leon, Mexico, and specializes in selling esoteric products and services.

The aim of this prototype was to create a landing page for the business where certain information would be presented, such as about them, the services they offer, and contact information.

Tools: Figma, Canva.
