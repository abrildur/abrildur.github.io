Expert Evaluation of the Largest retail platform in South America

Éxito

Project Overview

Éxito is the largest leading retail platform in South America, with presence in Colombia and in Uruguay with Grupo Disco and Grupo Devoto. It is also present in Argentina with Libertad and Paseo.

Project duration: 1 week.

The aim of this study was to recognize and demonstrate the usability problems of one of Colombia's largest online stores.

Team: 5 UX Researchers

My role: UX Researcher

Responsibilities:

  • Expert Evaluator
  • Design Research
  • Content Creator

Challenges:

  • Collaboration and adaptation for reporting results.
Full report (spanish)

Methodology

Research

Understanding the company and it's audience

The following question guided my use of Éxito website during the day I spent:

"What would a user want to achieve with a website like this?"

Upon doing this research, I analyzed Éxito's competition and also identified the company's value proposition.
